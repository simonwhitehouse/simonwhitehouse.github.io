Welcome to BeforeCast

BeforeCast is your ultimate fishing weather app, designed to help anglers make informed decisions with real-time data. Get accurate weather forecasts, wind direction, air pressure, and moon phases for your current location or any specified location. The app uses your device's location locally to fetch data from Apple's WeatherKit API, ensuring your location stays private and never leaves your device.
